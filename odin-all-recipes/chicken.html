Chicken Stir-Fry

What it is:

A quick and healthy dish with chicken and vegetables in a savory sauce, served with rice.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced into thin strips)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Cooked rice (for serving)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

How to make it:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned and fully cooked, about 5-7 minutes. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add sesame oil and saut√© garlic for about 1 minute.
  4. Add the bell pepper, onion, and zucchini. Cook for about 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  5. Return the chicken to the pan and pour in the soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir everything together.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken slightly.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve with rice.
