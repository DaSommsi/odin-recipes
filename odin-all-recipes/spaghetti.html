Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

What it is:

A simple Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, chili flakes, and parsley.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves of garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

How to make it:

  • Cook the spaghetti in salted water according to the package instructions.
  • While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add the garlic and chili flakes, saut√©ing until the garlic turns golden but not burnt (about 2 minutes).
  • Drain the pasta, saving a cup of pasta water.
  • Add the cooked pasta to the garlic-oil mixture, tossing well. If needed, add a little pasta water to help combine.
  • Stir in fresh parsley and season with salt.
  • Serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese (optional).
